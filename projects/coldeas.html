Coldeas

Created: 23 Dec 2013

Stage: Concept

Currently a prospective project inspired by a high school teacher and their passion for collaborative learning. It is a project akin to Knowledge Forum that is designed to be open and easy for teachers to learn and implement within their respective classrooms. As an open source project it will be open to contributions from educators all over to host features that they find most beneficial to their students learning.
